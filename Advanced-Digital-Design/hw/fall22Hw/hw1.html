Homework #1

Guidelines
  • Come prepared to answer question at the start of class on Canvas.


  1. Design a digital system with four bits of inputs I3 I2 I1 I0 and two bits of outputs O1 O0. At least one of the inputs is always equal to 1. The output encodes the index of the most significant 1 in the input. For example, if I3 I2 I1 I0 = 0101, then the index of the most significant 1 is 2, hence O1 O0 = 10. Hint, you will need a don't care somewhere.
    1. Complete truth table.
    2. VHDL code for the circuit using behavorial model.
