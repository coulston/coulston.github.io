Overview

Today we will dive into the operational principles of the 555 timer and develope an equivlant circuit that we will use to predict the the behavior of a 555 timer wired to discrete components.

During this week you will be wiring up a 555 timer on a bread board to make sure that the schematic functions properly. This prototype will allow you to determine is there are any needed changes to the circuit prior to laying out your PCB.

You will find that a well wires breadbord pays dividens later when you may be trouble shooting your circuit soldered onto a PCB.
