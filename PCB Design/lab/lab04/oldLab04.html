Epilog Laser Visit

Next week we will be traveling to Epilog Laser (16371 Table Mountain Parkway, Golden CO 80403) to talk with Allen Blaylock, lead engineer at Epilog Laser.

Allen will show us the design and manufacturing capabilities of Epilog and answer some questions from you. As part of your homework this week, I would like you to come up with three questions related to engineering a laser cutter/engraver. As source material, please consult Epilog's website to understand
  • PCB Design (Tools, boards, vendors)
  • Supply chain (choosing, history, grey market, cost)
  • Manufacturing
  • Debugging and Design stratigies
Epilog Laser is really engaged with Mines and more specifically the Electrical Engineering department. They are the primary sponsor for the Brown 305 lab - look at the plaque next time you enter the lab. Epilog Laser has also provided Mines with some really nice hardware in the Mines Garage. I would like our visit to leave a good impression so that we can continue this partnership in the coming years.
