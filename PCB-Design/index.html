Counterfeit

IC counterfeiting is becoming an increase problem in electronics manufacturing supply chains. This emphasizes the need to purchase from reputable vendors and test all products thoroughly before shipping.
  • ATmega counterfeits
  • Counterfeit Electronic Component Detection
