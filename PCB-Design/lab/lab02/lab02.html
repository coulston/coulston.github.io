    Lab 2 assignment

    You may complete this assignment in teams of two. Complete the layout of the VU meter by moving components and then routing the nets.
    • Move resistors into the two boxes labeled "Put Ra-Rb in here",
    • Leave the LM324, LEDs, and potentiometer where they are.
    • Orient resistors to minimize trace length,
    • Route the nets using 16 mil traces,
    • Avoid right angles on traces,
    • Do not route wires on bottom layer (no vias),
    • Delete my instructions and boxes,
    • Keep component designators (e.g. R1) and component values,
    • Run the DRC with the vuMeter Design Rules rule set provided to you in the inLab. Clear all warning and errors.
    • (Bonus) Add Blaster to layer 21 using the import-bmp ULP. Make sure to remove his background, just Blaster.
    • Print your layout to PDF as follows:
      • File → Print,
      • In the Print pop-up,
        • Printer: Microsoft Print to PDF
        • Orientation: Landscape
        • Alignment: Center
        • Area: Full
        • Scale factor: 3
      • Click OK,
      • In the "Save Print Output As" pop-up, navigate to your lab02 directory and give your file a meaningful name.
    • Upload your PDF to Canvas in the "Lab 2 - VU meter layout" assignment.
