Final Project Milestone #1

The lab professor and instructor will check your progress next week. This check will include collecting the results of your test plan in the form of a test report for milestone I. The test report should contain:
  • A title page (see posted final project guideline),
  • Level-1 block diagram (from your project plan),

The instructor will walk around, check functionality, and sign-off on the tests. You can demo your system live or use include pictures, videos, oscilloscope traces, or measurements. Make sure to keep these so you can possibly include them in your final project write-up and presentation.
